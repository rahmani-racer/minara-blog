Forex Knowledge

Forex market basics se advance concepts tak — simple Hindi/Hinglish me, beginners ke liye clear explanation ke saath.

Forex Market (Foreign Exchange) duniya ka sabse bada aur most liquid financial market hai, jiska daily volume $7 trillion+ hota hai.

Forex me trading currency pairs me hoti hai jaise EUR/USD, GBP/USD, USD/JPY aur USD/INR. Market 24/5 open rehta hai.

Jab aap ek currency buy karte ho, to dusri currency automatically sell hoti hai. Price movement ko pips me measure kiya jaata hai.

Forex analysis do types ka hota hai: Technical Analysis aur Fundamental Analysis.

Technical analysis me indicators jaise RSI, MACD, Moving Averages, aur support–resistance levels use hote hain.

Fundamental analysis me economic factors jaise interest rates, inflation, GDP aur NFP market direction ko impact karte hain.

Leverage ek powerful tool hai, lekin bina proper risk management ke ye account destroy kar sakta hai.

Professional traders hamesha stop-loss aur proper risk-to-reward ratio follow karte hain.

Forex me success discipline, patience aur consistent strategy se aati hai. Ye gambling nahi, ek long-term skill hai.
