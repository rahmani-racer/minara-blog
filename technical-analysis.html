Technical Analysis – Trading Foundation

Technical analysis ka matlab hota hai past price data ka use karke future movement ka estimate lagana.

Basic assumptions

Price sab kuch reflect karta hai, history repeat hoti hai, aur market trends me move karta hai.

Charts & Trends

Candlestick chart sabse powerful hota hai. Trend ke against trade karna beginners ke liye risky hota hai.

Indicators ka role

RSI, MACD aur moving averages support tools hain — decision maker nahi.

Patterns

Chart patterns probability batate hain, certainty nahi.

Risk Control

Technical analysis bina stop loss ke incomplete hai.

Conclusion

Technical analysis ek toolset hai, magic nahi.
