Taxes and Trading

Last Updated: 16 Jan 2026

Intro

This is a high-level guide on record-keeping and tax considerations for traders in India. Consult a qualified tax advisor for personalised advice.

Key points

  1. Keep thorough trade and P&L records for reporting.
  2. Understand differences between business income vs capital gains.

GOLD example

Record your XAU/USD trades, fees, and conversions carefully; include screenshots and statements for audit trails.

Risk warning

This page is educational only and not tax advice. Talk to a local tax professional for compliance.
