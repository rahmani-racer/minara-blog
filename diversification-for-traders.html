Diversification for Traders

Last Updated: 16 Jan 2026

Intro

Diversification reduces idiosyncratic risk but can dilute your best ideas; balance concentration and diversification.

Step-by-step

  1. Decide how many distinct uncorrelated setups you can manage well.
  2. Limit cluster exposure to avoid accidental concentration.

GOLD example

If you trade XAU/USD primarily, consider smaller positions in non-correlated FX pairs to diversify regime exposure.
