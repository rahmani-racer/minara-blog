Beginner Forex Mistakes

Last Updated: 16 Jan 2026 — Minara Blog

Problem-based introduction

Har beginner se common mistakes hoti hain—over-leverage, no stop-loss, lack of plan. Yeh article clear corrections aur GOLD examples de kar help karega avoid karne mein.

Step-by-step explanation

  1. Define a trading plan with clear entry, stop and target.
  2. Use position sizing based on risk per trade rather than guesswork.
  3. Maintain a trading journal to improve over time.

Real trading logic (GOLD example)

Example mistake: No stop-loss on a GOLD breakout—result large drawdown. Correction: predefine stop and size position to limit loss to tolerated % of account.

Image-based examples (mandatory)

Common Mistakes

  • Overleverage
  • No defined stop-loss
  • Ignoring costs and slippage
  • Not journaling trades

Pro Tips

  • Start with a plan and risk limits for every trade.
  • Review trades weekly and learn from mistakes rather than repeating them.

Risk Warning

Trading is risky. Avoid risking essential funds and practice disciplined risk management.

SEO FAQs

1. Sabse common beginner mistake kya hai?
Over-leverage and lack of stop-loss are most common and dangerous.
2. Kaise improve karein?
Follow a plan, use demo, journal every trade, and manage risk strictly.
