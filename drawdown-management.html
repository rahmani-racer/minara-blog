Drawdown Management

Last Updated: 16 Jan 2026

Intro

Drawdowns are inevitable. How you respond—rule-based adjustments and discipline—matters most.

Step-by-step

  1. Track peak-to-trough and define an acceptable drawdown threshold.
  2. Reduce risk per trade when drawdown exceeds thresholds.
  3. Review strategy and market regime; adjust or pause if edge is gone.

GOLD example

During a losing stretch on XAU/USD, cut risk by 50% after a predefined drawdown limit and review trade logs for regime mismatches.

Common mistakes

  • Chasing higher returns immediately after a drawdown.
