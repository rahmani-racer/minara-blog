Handling Trading Emotions

Last Updated: 16 Jan 2026

Intro

Emotions drive mistakes. Learn practical mental checks and pre-trade routines to limit emotional decisions.

Step-by-step

  1. Use a checklist before every trade (setup, risk, reason, plan).
  2. Set alarms for when you deviate from the plan and take a break.
  3. Keep position size small when feeling emotional.

GOLD example

If you feel FOMO after a move in XAU/USD, step back—do not increase size or chase entries without plan-based confirmation.
