Forex Trading – Complete Beginner Guide

Forex trading ka matlab hota hai ek currency ko buy karna aur doosri currency ko sell karna. Ye duniya ka sabse bada financial market hai.

Forex Market kaise kaam karta hai?

Forex market 24 hours, 5 din open rehta hai. Ye decentralized market hai jahan banks, institutions aur retail traders sab participate karte hain.

Currency Pairs Explained

Forex me hamesha pairs me trade hota hai jaise EUR/USD. Pehli currency base hoti hai aur doosri quote currency.

Major Trading Sessions

  • Asian Session
  • London Session
  • New York Session

Leverage aur Risk

Leverage profit badha sakta hai, lekin bina risk management ke account destroy bhi kar sakta hai.

Beginner Rules

  • Demo account se start karo
  • Small lot size rakho
  • Stop loss zaroor lagao

Conclusion

Forex ek long-term skill hai. Discipline, patience aur risk control ke saath hi success possible hai.
