Best Time to Trade Forex

Last Updated: 16 Jan 2026 — Minara Blog

Problem-based introduction

Traders ask "kab trade karun" — answer depends on instrument and strategy. Is GOLD better in London hours? We'll explain session overlaps and practical timing for Indian traders.

Step-by-step explanation

  1. Define your strategy: scalping, intraday, swing—each prefers different session windows.
  2. Understand session overlaps: London–New York overlap gives best liquidity for majors and GOLD.
  3. Avoid low-liquidity times for high-frequency strategies.

Real trading logic (GOLD example)

For GOLD, London and New York sessions see most activity. Plan entries during overlap or post-confirmation to reduce slippage and get better fills.

Image-based examples (mandatory)

Common Mistakes

  • Using the same timing for all instruments without adaptation.
  • Trading news without adjusting size for volatility.

Pro Tips

  • Backtest your strategy on the specific session you plan to trade.
  • Keep an eye on economic calendar for session-specific volatility spikes.

Risk Warning

Session behaviour changes during holidays and geopolitical events; adjust plans accordingly.

SEO FAQs

1. Best time for GOLD trading?
London–New York overlap typically has best liquidity for GOLD.
2. Kya Asian session trade karna bekar hai?
Not necessarily; but choose lower sizes and be aware of spreads.
