Price Action Basics

Last Updated: 16 Jan 2026

Intro

Price action is reading raw price — candles, levels, momentum without heavy reliance on indicators.

The philosophy: Indicators lagging hain—price pehle move karta hai, indicator baad me react karta hai. Price action real-time market behaviour hai—supply/demand zones, momentum shifts, institutional footprints. Traders learn direct market language, not indicator translations.

Why it works: Markets move on order flow (buyers vs sellers). Price action directly shows this battle—rejection wicks = demand exceeded supply. Engulfing candles = momentum shift. Indicators yeh information process karke lagging signal dete hain. Price action = leading information, faster reaction possible.

Clean chart advantage: 50 indicators = analysis paralysis. Conflicting signals = confusion. Naked chart (price + key levels) = clarity. Decision-making fast ho jata hai. Institutional traders price action use karte hain—indicators retail distraction hain primarily.

Step-by-step

  1. HTF structure analysis (Daily/4H): Identify trend direction—higher highs + higher lows = uptrend. Mark last major swing high aur swing low. GOLD daily pe 2050 support aur 2150 resistance visible—range define karo pehle.
  2. Key zone marking: Support/resistance zones draw karo (3-5 touches ideal). Round numbers bhi mark karo (GOLD 2100, 2000). Institutional levels hote hain. Agar price 3rd time zone test kare to high-probability setup hai.
  3. LTF entry refinement (1H/15min): HTF structure decide karne ke baad LTF pe clean setup wait karo. Retest, pinbar, engulfing candle. Entry precise hona chahiye—HTF bias + LTF trigger combination.
  4. Candlestick pattern recognition: Bullish engulfing (previous red candle ko green candle fully cover kare), bearish pinbar (long upper wick, small body), rejection wicks (zone test karke reject). Context matters—trend me continuation patterns strong hain.
  5. Risk-reward zone confirmation: Entry decide karne se pehle stop aur target measure karo. Agar 1:2 R:R nahi milta to skip. GOLD 2100 entry, 2080 stop, 2140 target = 1:2 valid. Forced trades mat lo.

GOLD example

Setup: GOLD daily chart uptrend (higher highs since 2000). Price pullback to 2100 support zone (3rd touch—previous bounces at 2098, 2102). Daily candle forms bullish engulfing—red candle $15 range, next candle green $30 range covering previous fully. Volume spike confirmation.

Entry decision: Engulfing close = 2110. Stop below zone low = 2080 (30-pip risk = $300 standard lot). Target previous high = 2160 (50 pips = $500). R:R = 1:1.67 (acceptable for high-probability retest). 4H chart shows no resistance till 2140—clean path.

Execution: 1H chart pe wait karo engulfing candle close hone ke baad. Agar next candle bullish continuation dikhe (green candle higher high banaye) to enter. Agar doji ya bearish reversal aaye to skip—false breakout possible. Patience > FOMO.

Image example

CoRushing entries without structure: Candle pattern dekha aur immediately enter kar diya bina zone confirmation ke. Pinbar support zone pe powerful hai, mid-air pinbar meaningless. Context > pattern.
  • HTF ignore karke LTF obsession: 15-min chart pe bullish setup dekha but daily chart downtrend me hai. LTF noise hai, HTF direction priority. Against-trend trades low probability.
  • Indicator dependency: Price action me RSI, MACD lagana zaroori nahi. Naked chart overwhelming lagta hai initially but practice se clarity aati hai. Candles + levels = enough.
  • Pattern over-recognition: Har candle me pattern dhundhna—"yeh inverse head-and-shoulders lag raha hai". Complex patterns unreliable hain. Simple setups (engulfing, pinbar, retest) consistent hote hain.
  • Volume ignore: Breakout volume check nahi karna. Low-volume breakout = false breakout high probability. Institutional moves volume ke saath aati hain. Volume spike = conviction signal.
  • Multi-timeframe confusion: Daily uptrend, 4H downtrend, 1H uptrend—contradiction me entry kar di. Alignment chahiye: Daily + 4H same direction, 1H trigger. Conflicting signals = skip
    • Rushing entries without structure confirmation.

    • Screenshot discipline: Har setup ka screenshot lo—entry reason, HTF context, LTF trigger. Review karo weekend pe. Pattern recognition muscle memory se aati hai, repetition se.
  • Zone strength ranking: Support/resistance zones ko rate karo: 3+ touches = A-grade, 2 touches = B-grade, untested zone = C-grade. A-grade zones pe aggressive, C-grade skip ya small size.
  • Confluence hunting: Price action alone good hai, confluence ke saath powerful. Zone + round number + Fibonacci 61.8% + previous day close = high conviction. Confluence = probability multiplier.
  • Session timing awareness: London open pe breakouts real hote hain (volume), Asian session me fake breakouts common. GOLD Asia session me tight range, London open me direction decide hota hai. Timing edge hai.
  • Rejection wick size rule: Pinbar wick body ka 2× hona chahiye minimum. Small wick = weak rejection. GOLD 2100 test, wick $15, body $5 = strong rejection. Wick $8, body $6 = weak, skip.
  • Daily bias ritual: Har morning 5 min laga ke daily chart dekho—trend, zones, overnight action. Bias set karo (bullish/bearish/neutral). Intraday trades bias ke saath align karke lo. Conviction = consistency

    • Use price action first, indicators second.
    • Journal setups with screenshots to build recognition.

    simple terms me?
    Market ka asli behaviour—candles aur levels—bina heavy indicators ke. Traders price movement se supply-demand zones aur momentum samajhte hain. Clean chart philosophy hai—distractions kam, clarity zyada.
    Price action vs indicators—better kaun hai?
    Price action leading hai (real-time market behaviour), indicators lagging (past data calculations). Indicators supporting role me acche hain (RSI divergence, MA trend confirmation) but primary signal price action se aana chahiye. Priority: Price > Indicators.
    HTF aur LTF alignment kaise check karun?
    Daily chart dekho—uptrend hai to long bias. 4H chart dekho—agar pullback support pe hai to high probability. 1H chart pe bullish engulfing ya pinbar wait karo. Alignment = Daily + 4H same direction, 1H trigger. Conflicting signals me skip.
    Support/resistance zones kitne wide hone chahiye?
    GOLD me 10-20 pips zone width realistic hai—2100-2110 support zone. Tight line (2105 exact) unrealistic hai, price fluctuate karta hai. Zone concept flexible hai—price zone me kahi bhi bounce kar sakta hai. 3-5 pip buffer dena zaroori hai.
    Pinbar aur engulfing me difference kya hai?
    Pinbar = long wick (rejection), small body. Resistance test karke reject = bearish pinbar. Engulfing = previous candle ko fully cover karna. Red candle ke baad green candle larger = bullish engulfing. Dono reversal signals, context important (zone pe powerful).
    Volume kaise check karun MT4/MT5 me?
    MT4 me volume indicator by default available hai (Insert > Indicators > Volumes). Breakout candle ka volume previous 10 candles se 2× hona chahiye = genuine move. Low volume breakout = false breakout probability high. Institutional moves volume ke saath aati hain.
    Price action me stop-loss kaha lagaun?
    Zone ke niche/upar logical point pe. Support zone 2100-2110 hai to stop 2095 (zone + 5 pip buffer). Structure-based stops market-driven hote hain. Fixed pip stops arbitrary hain (say 50 pips har trade me)—ATR + zone combination use karo.
    Multi-timeframe analysis time-consuming hai—zaroori hai kya?
    Absolutely. Daily 5 min lagaon—HTF trend clarity deta hai, LTF timing refine karta hai. Ek timeframe me trade karna = limited context = low probability. Daily direction + 4H zones + 1H trigger = high conviction setups. Time investment justified hai.
    Price action learning me kitna time lagta hai?
    Basic patterns 2-3 months me samajh aa jate hain. Consistent execution 6-12 months practice. Screenshot discipline + journal mandatory—100-200 setups review karne se pattern recognition automatic ho jata hai. Backtesting + live practice = muscle memory.
    Indicators bilkul use nahi karne chahiye price action me?
    Use kar sakte ho supporting role me—not primary signal. Example: Daily price action bullish, RSI oversold confirmation = higher conviction. But RSI oversold alone = insufficient. Price action primary, indicators secondary. Max 1-2 indicators—50 EMA + RSI enough.

    Risk warning

    Price action timing improve karta hai but markets still gap kar sakti hain, slippage ho sakta hai. Always proper position size use karo—clean setup + wrong size = risk uncontrolled. News events se 30 minutes pehle aur baad avoid trading—price action reliable nahi hota extreme volatility me.

    Pattern reliability limits: Pinbar, engulfing, rejection wicks—yeh patterns 60-70% win-rate provide karte hain best conditions me (HTF alignment, zone confluence). But 30-40% time fail bhi karte hain. Pattern dekh ke blindly entry mat lo—context mandatory (trend, zone, volume). Pattern alone insufficient hai.

    Subjectivity challenge: Price action subjective hai partially—do traders same chart me different levels draw kar sakte hain. Consistency build karne me 6-12 months practice lagta hai. Backtesting + screenshot journal mandatory—pattern recognition muscle memory se aati hai. Agar tum daily inconsistent zones draw kar rahe ho to live trading se pehle demo me consistency build karo.
