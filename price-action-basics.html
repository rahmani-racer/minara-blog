Price Action Basics

Last Updated: 16 Jan 2026

Intro

Price action is reading raw price — candles, levels, momentum without heavy reliance on indicators.

Step-by-step

  1. Identify higher-timeframe trend structure.
  2. Mark swing highs/lows and key zones.
  3. Wait for clean setups on lower timeframes—retests, pinbars, engulfing candles.

GOLD example

On XAU/USD daily, a clear support zone followed by a bullish engulfing daily gives a logical long with defined stop below the zone.

Image example

Common mistakes

  • Rushing entries without structure confirmation.

Pro tips

  • Use price action first, indicators second.
  • Journal setups with screenshots to build recognition.

Risk warning

Price action improves timing but markets can still gap; always size properly.

FAQs

Price action kya hai?
Market ka asli behaviour—candles aur levels—bina heavy indicators ke.
