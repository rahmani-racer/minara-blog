Trading Journal Practices

Last Updated: 16 Jan 2026

Intro

A journal is the fastest route to improvement—track setups, emotions, screenshots, and outcomes.

Step-by-step

  1. Record setup, entry, stop, size, and screenshots.
  2. Tag trades by setup type and outcome.
  3. Review weekly and fix recurring mistakes.

GOLD example

Log an XAU/USD breakout trade with pre/post screenshots and notes about what went right/wrong.

Pro tips

  • Keep the journal simple and consistent; avoid long essays.
