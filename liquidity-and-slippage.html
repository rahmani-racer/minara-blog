Liquidity and Slippage

Last Updated: 16 Jan 2026 — Minara Blog

Problem-based introduction

Liquidity aur slippage samajhna zaroori hai warna entry/exit price unexpected ho sakta hai. Is article mein simple Hinglish mein liquidity pools, market depth aur slippage kaise kam karein, GOLD examples ke sath discuss karenge.

Step-by-step explanation

  1. Liquidity: Availability of counterpart orders at various price levels.
  2. Market depth: Order book size at each price level affects fill quality.
  3. Slippage: Difference between expected price and executed price; increases in low liquidity or volatile times.

Real trading logic (GOLD example)

During news, XAU/USD spreads widen and slippage can be larger. Use limit orders, smaller sizes, or avoid trading during such windows to reduce slippage risk.

Image-based examples (mandatory)

Common Mistakes

  • Trading large size in thin markets without checking depth.
  • Using market orders in volatile news events expecting small slippage.

Pro Tips

  • Measure typical slippage for your broker during target hours and factor it into trade planning.
  • Prefer limit entries for structured setups to control fills.

Risk Warning

Slippage can change P&L significantly; manage size and avoid trading during extreme events unless strategy accounts for it.

SEO FAQs

1. Slippage kaise reduce karein?
Use limit orders, smaller sizes, and avoid low-liquidity windows.
2. Liquidity kaise check karein?
Check spreads, depth (if available), and compare during target sessions.
3. Kya slippage broker ka fault hota hai?
Sometimes execution model affects slippage, but market conditions are primary driver.
