Writing a Trading Plan

Last Updated: 16 Jan 2026

Intro

A plan turns intuition into rules. A good plan covers entry, sizing, exits, risk, and review routines.

Step-by-step

  1. Define mission and time commitment.
  2. List setups, sizing rules, stop rules, and routine checks.
  3. Include contingency rules for black swan events.

GOLD example

Sample plan: only trade XAU/USD breakouts on daily charts, risk 1% per trade, partial exits at 1R.

Pro tips

  • Revisit and update the plan after every 3 months or significant strategy change.
