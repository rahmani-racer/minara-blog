Price Action Trading – Complete Guide

Price Action Trading ka matlab hota hai sirf price ko padhna aur samajhna — bina unnecessary indicators ke. Is approach me trader market ke raw movement par focus karta hai.

Price Action ka core concept

Market me jo bhi ho raha hota hai — news, fear, greed — sab kuch price me already reflect hota hai. Isliye price action trader indicators ke peeche nahi bhagta.

Market Structure

Higher highs aur higher lows = uptrend. Lower highs aur lower lows = downtrend. Structure clear ho to decision simple hota hai.

Support & Resistance ke saath Price Action

Price action lines nahi, zones pe kaam karta hai. Jahan price baar-baar react kare, wahi decision area hota hai.

Candlestick Signals

Pin bar, engulfing aur inside bar jaise patterns sirf confirmation dete hain, guarantee nahi.

Risk Management

Best setup bhi fail ho sakta hai. Isliye stop loss aur fixed risk mandatory hai.

Conclusion

Price action ek skill hai jo patience aur practice se develop hoti hai. Shortcut yahan kaam nahi karta.
