Choosing a Broker

Last Updated: 16 Jan 2026

Intro

Broker choice affects costs and execution—compare spreads, commissions, execution speed, and client support before committing.

Checklist

  1. Compare all-in costs including spreads and overnight swaps.
  2. Test platform stability and execution on demo accounts.

GOLD example

For XAU/USD traders, prioritize low swap rates if you carry positions and reliable execution during volatile sessions.
