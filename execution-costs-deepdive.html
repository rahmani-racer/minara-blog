Execution Costs Deep Dive

Last Updated: 16 Jan 2026

Intro

Execution costs can erode returns. Model realistic spreads and slippage when evaluating strategies.

Modeling tips

  1. Include average spread, commission, and slippage per trade.
  2. Stress-test execution during low-liquidity events.
